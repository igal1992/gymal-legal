# Gymal Support

**Last updated:** 20 May 2026

Need help with Gymal? Here's how to get in touch and what to try first.

## Contact us

**Email:** [gymonzapp@gmail.com](mailto:gymonzapp@gmail.com)

We aim to respond within **2 business days**. When you write, please include:

- The device you're using (e.g. iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S24)
- The app version (Settings → About in the app)
- A clear description of what happened and what you expected

Screenshots are very helpful for visual bugs.

## Common questions

### How do I delete my account?

See the [Account & Data Deletion](account-deletion.html) page. The fastest way is from inside the app: **Settings → Account → Delete account**.

### I forgot my password.

On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password?** and enter your email. We'll send you a password reset link from Firebase Authentication.

### My workout streak reset and I don't think it should have.

Streaks reset if a training day passes without a logged workout. Make sure your training-days-per-week setting in **Settings → Workout** matches the days you actually train. You can also buy a **Streak Freeze** from the in-app shop to protect a missed day.

### A purchase didn't go through but my card was charged.

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple or Google directly — we don't see your payment details. If the entitlement (Pro, Max, or a coin pack) didn't appear:

1. Restart the app.
2. On iOS, go to **Settings → Account → Restore Purchases**.
3. If it's still not showing within an hour, email us with your purchase receipt from Apple or Google and we'll investigate.

For refunds, contact Apple ([reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)) or Google Play directly — they manage the billing.

### How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can submit feedback from **Settings → Offer Feedback** inside the app, or email us at the address above. Bug reports with reproduction steps and screenshots get fixed fastest.

### How do I report another player?

Tap the player's name in your friends list or on the leaderboard and choose **Report**. Reports are reviewed and we'll act on display names or behavior that violates our [Terms of Service](https://igal1992.github.io/gymal-legal/).
